Delete Your Furas Account

Last updated: May 2026

You can delete your Furas account and all associated data directly from within the app at any time. No need to contact us — deletion is immediate and permanent.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Furas app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap Account Settings.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and immediately removed:

  • Your account — your phone number and login credentials
  • Your job listings — all jobs you have posted as an employer
  • Your saved jobs — all job listings you have bookmarked

What May Be Retained

  • Anonymized analytics data — usage event data (e.g., screens visited, buttons tapped) held by third-party analytics providers (Mixpanel, Firebase) may be retained per their own retention policies. This data contains no name or phone number and cannot be linked back to you.
  • Crash and error reports — anonymized technical error logs held by Sentry may be retained for a limited period for debugging purposes. These contain no personal information.

No Retention Period

All personal data (profile, job listings, saved jobs, and authentication credentials) is deleted immediately upon account deletion. There is no grace period or recovery option — deletion is permanent.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access your account to delete it, contact us at support@furasapp.com and we will delete your account and data manually within 7 days.
