Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Furas ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Furas mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the App's services:

  • Phone number — used to create and authenticate your account via SMS verification code. We do not store your phone number in plain text outside of your profile.
  • Profile information — for employers: full name, business name, city, and business type. This is entered voluntarily when creating an account.
  • Job listings — content you post as an employer, including job title, description, pay rate, location, and contact phone number.
  • Saved jobs — if you save a job listing, we store a copy of that listing linked to your account so you can access it later.
  • Device location — requested optionally when you use the map view to find nearby jobs. Location is never stored on our servers.
  • Language preference — stored locally on your device to remember your English or Arabic setting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you securely via SMS one-time password (OTP)
  • To display your employer profile and job listings to job seekers
  • To save and retrieve your bookmarked job listings
  • To show nearby jobs on the map view (location is used in real time and never saved)
  • To allow other users to contact you directly via phone or WhatsApp if you post a job

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Your data is shared only in the following limited ways:

  • Supabase — our backend database and authentication provider. Supabase stores your profile data and job listings securely. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Other users — if you post a job as an employer, your business name, job details, and contact phone number are visible to anyone browsing the App.

4. Data Retention

  • Job listings are automatically removed after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Your account and profile data are retained until you delete your account.
  • You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab in the App. Deletion permanently removes your profile, job listings, and saved jobs.

5. Children's Privacy

Furas is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@furasapp.com.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and Supabase's Row Level Security (RLS) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information via the Account Settings screen in the App
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy questions or requests

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:

Email: support@furasapp.com
App: Furas — available on the App Store and Google Play
